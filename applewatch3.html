Series 3

The series 3 watch has a faster processor, the dual-core S3,Bluetooth 4.2, a built-in altimeter,and LTE is avaliable. The biggest change is the addition of cellular connectivity. Apple Watch users are no longer needed to be nearby to their iPhone; instead, they can remain connected, make and receive calls, stream music, send and receive texts, and more without having their iPhone nearby. Series 3 models have a faster dual-core processor and barometric altimeter. Series 3 devices also offer new styles and bands made by apple.
