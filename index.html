The Apple Watch

The Apple watch is a watch made by apple that will work with your iphone. The first generation was released april 2015.Apple has made series 1 through 7 so far.

  • Series 1

    • The series 1 watch uses a varient of the dual-core Apple S2 processor with the GPS removed.

  • Series 2

    • The series 2 also uses the dual-core Apple S2 processor,it uses the GPS reciver and is water resistant up to 50 meters.

  • Series 3

    • The series 3 watch has a faster processor, the dual-core S3,Bluetooth 4.2, a built-in altimeter,and LTE is avaliable.

  • Series 4

    • The series 4 watch has a bigger display, a new 64-bit dual core processor upgraded 16 GB storage, new options for watch face customization, and a new heart sensor.

  • Series 5

    • The series 5 has a more efficient S5 processor, improved ambient light sensor, and storage doubled to 32 GB.
