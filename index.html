The Apple Watch

The Apple watch is a watch made by apple that will work with your iphone. The first generation was released april 2015.Apple has made series 1 through 7 so far.

  • Series 1

    • The series 1 watch uses a varient of the dual-core Apple S2 processor with the GPS removed.

  • Series 2

    • The series 2 also uses the dual-core Apple S2 processor,it uses the GPS reciver and is water resistant up to 50 meters.

  • Series 3

    • The series 3 watch has a faster processor, the dual-core S3,Bluetooth 4.2, a built-in altimeter,and LTE is avaliable.

  • Series 4

    • The series 4 watch has a bigger display, a new 64-bit dual core processor upgraded 16 GB storage, new options for watch face customization, and a new heart sensor.

  • Series 5

    • The series 5 has a more efficient S5 processor, improved ambient light sensor, and storage doubled to 32 GB.

    The Apple watch comes in many different varieties of color, casing material and size. There is also countless variety of watch bands that you can choose from and buy from many different websites. Originally Apple made three different collections which are made out of different materials such as Aluminum case for the Apple Watch sport, Stainless steel case for the Apple Watch, and Apple Watch Edition an 18kt gold casing with newer materials in later models for the Apple Watch Edition The smaller size at launch was 38 mm (1.5 in) while the larger size was 42 mm (1.7 in). Now in the Series 4 and later, the two sizes 40 mm (1.6 in) (smaller) and 44 mm (1.7 in) (larger). The watch includes a "digital crown" which you can use to scroll or zoom when turned and when pressed it sends you back to the home screen. Apple Watch runs watchOS which is the watch equivalent to iOS. The Apple Watch is capable of receiving notifications, messages, and phone calls when paired with an iPhone. Apple Watch also supports Apple Pay. Analysts estimate that Apple sold 18 million watches in 2017, 31 million in 2019, and 34 million in 2020. In 2021 there were 100 million watches in use.
