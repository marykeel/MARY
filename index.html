The Apple Watch

The Apple watch is a watch made by apple that will work with your iphone. The first generation was released april 2015.Apple has made series 1 through 7 so far.

  • Series 1

    • The series 1 watch uses a varient of the dual-core Apple S2 processor with the GPS removed.When you recive a apple watch series 1, a few diffrent things will come in the box. Some of these are Apple Watch , Magnetic charging cable, USB power adapter, Band (Sport Band includes band that can be used for either S/M or M/L length), and Quick start guide. Apple Watch is splash and water resistant but not waterproof. The Apple watch series 1 features; Stainless steel or space black stainless steel case Sapphire crystal, Retina display with Force Touch, Ceramic back, Digital Crown, Heart rate sensor, accelerometer, gyroscope, Ambient light sensor, Speaker and microphone, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Up to 18 hours of battery life, Water resistant, and watchOS 2.

  • Series 2

    • The series 2 also uses the dual-core Apple S2 processor,it uses the GPS reciver and is water resistant up to 50 meters. This means that it may be used for shallow-water activities like swimming in a pool or ocean. Some power and battery features are Built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery, Up to 18 hours, Magnetic charging cable, and a USB power adapter. The regular apple watch comes with an Aluminum case with Sport Band or Woven Nylon band and a Stainless steel case with Sport Band, stainless steel band, or leather band.

  • Series 3

    • The series 3 watch has a faster processor, the dual-core S3,Bluetooth 4.2, a built-in altimeter,and LTE is avaliable. The biggest change is the addition of cellular connectivity. Apple Watch users are no longer needed to be nearby to their iPhone; instead, they can remain connected, make and receive calls, stream music, send and receive texts, and more without having their iPhone nearby. Series 3 models have a faster dual-core processor and barometric altimeter. Series 3 devices also offer new styles and bands made by apple.

  • Series 4

    • The series 4 watch has a bigger display, a new 64-bit dual core processor upgraded 16 GB storage, new options for watch face customization, and a new heart sensor.The Apple Watch Series 4 comes in two sizes (40mm and 44mm) and also brings along a 30 percent larger display. the Apple Watch Series 4 has the same 18-hour, all-day battery life. Apple has increased outdoor workout time to six hours, with full GPS tracking for long bike rides and or runs.The Apple Watch Series 4 is available for purchase and starts at $399, while the cellular model starts at $499.

  • Series 5

    • The series 5 has a more efficient S5 processor, improved ambient light sensor, and storage doubled to 32 GB.Apple Watch Series 5 features a new display that allows the time and important information to be visible at all times.For added safety, users with cellular models of Apple Watch Series 5 can now complete international calls to emergency services, regardless of where the device was originally purchased or if the cellular plan has been activated.

    The Apple watch comes in many different varieties of color, casing material and size. There is also countless variety of watch bands that you can choose from and buy from many different websites. Originally Apple made three different collections which are made out of different materials such as Aluminum case for the Apple Watch sport, Stainless steel case for the Apple Watch, and Apple Watch Edition an 18kt gold casing with newer materials in later models for the Apple Watch Edition The smaller size at launch was 38 mm (1.5 in) while the larger size was 42 mm (1.7 in). Now in the Series 4 and later, the two sizes 40 mm (1.6 in) (smaller) and 44 mm (1.7 in) (larger). The watch includes a "digital crown" which you can use to scroll or zoom when turned and when pressed it sends you back to the home screen. Apple Watch runs watchOS which is the watch equivalent to iOS. The Apple Watch is capable of receiving notifications, messages, and phone calls when paired with an iPhone. Apple Watch also supports Apple Pay. Analysts estimate that Apple sold 18 million watches in 2017, 31 million in 2019, and 34 million in 2020. In 2021 there were 100 million watches in use.
